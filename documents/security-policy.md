# Security Policy

## 1. Physical and Information Security
NileTech Solutions Ltd. maintains strict protocols to guard corporate property, software codebases, client data, and personnel files. 

## 2. Personnel Records and Data Privacy
* **Authorized Access**: The organization maintains modern, secure personnel records for each employee. Any person other than the concerned administrative human resource staff shall not have access to the personnel records of an employee without their explicit written consent, unless strictly authorized by a competent court order or explicit provision of the law.
* **Employee Rights**: Every employee has the right to access all information contained within their own personnel records or receive a verified copy thereof. 
* **Transparency of Records**: It is strictly prohibited to deposit any disciplinary document, performance review, or administrative file into an employee's personnel record without making it explicitly known or informed to the employee beforehand.

## 3. Workplace Environment Security
* **Neutral Workspace**: To preserve a safe and neutral workplace for all staff, any government or corporate office environment under our jurisdiction must remain completely free from any form of religious practices, religious activities, or the display of sectarian materials.
* **Equipment Safety**: Employees are required to promptly inform the designated security or operations officer of any physical condition, system vulnerability, or environmental hazard which they have reason to believe could present a hazard to data or personnel.
