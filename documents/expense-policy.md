# Expense Policy

## 1. Institutional Principles and Allowances
All business expenses and corporate allowances within NileTech Solutions Ltd. are authorized strictly for the purpose of carrying out the core functions of the organization. No discretionary expenses shall be reimbursed unless they directly support corporate operations, technological service delivery, or approved public service goals.

## 2. Expense Authorization and Reimbursement
* **Prior Approval**: Any operational or departmental expense must receive formal, written authorization from the department head prior to procurement.
* **Documentation**: Detailed invoices, receipts, and itemized bills must accompany all expense claims. File receipts must clearly indicate the volume, cost, and date of the transaction.
* **Deduction and Liabilities**: Any employee who intentionally misrepresents expenses, submits fraudulent receipts, or misallocates corporate funds shall be subject to rigorous disciplinary penalties, including formal dismissal, and faces civil liability for property damages under the company's financial protocols.
