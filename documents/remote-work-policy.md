# Remote Work Policy

## 1. Scope and Eligibility
NileTech Solutions Ltd. supports flexible, modern work arrangements to maximize operational execution, maintain institutional continuity, and enhance organizational efficiency. This policy applies to all full-time permanent employees who have successfully completed their probationary period, subject to role compatibility and departmental performance metrics.

## 2. Core Work Schedule and Availability
* **Flexible Allocation**: Eligible employees may work remotely for up to a maximum of three (3) days per week. All remote arrangements require advanced, written approval from the direct department manager.
* **Operational Alignment**: While working remotely, employees must strictly align their core hours of availability with the company's statutory 39-hour workweek. This includes availability from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM on Monday through Thursday, and during the split sessions on Friday (8:30 AM to 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM to 5:30 PM).
* **Communication Standards**: Remote workers are required to remain fully accessible via company-approved digital communication channels (e.g., email, chat, video conferencing) during these core hours.

## 3. Performance Tracking and Transparency
* **Evidence-Based Evaluation**: Remote work privileges are inherently tied to continuous, objective performance tracking. Remote employees are evaluated based on tangible work outcomes, target deadlines, volume, and quality of technical execution.
* **Privilege Revocation**: In line with company efficiency standards, remote work permissions may be suspended or fully revoked by management if an employee's performance appraisal falls below satisfactory or if the arrangement compromises the public or operational interest of the institution.
