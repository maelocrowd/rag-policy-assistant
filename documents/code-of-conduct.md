# Code of Conduct

## 1. Oath of Fidelity and Core Obligations
Every employee of NileTech Solutions Ltd. must maintain the highest standards of professional ethics, integrity, and public fidelity. Before commencing duties, all employees must adhere to the values contained in the formal Oath of Fidelity:
* **Fidelity**: Employees must remain loyal to the public interest, corporate strategies, and the national constitution.
* **Bias-Free Service**: Employees must serve the public, clients, and colleagues without any form of bias or discriminatory treatment on the basis of gender, language, ethnicity, religion, political stand, physical disability, or health status.
* **Religious Neutrality**: Employees must be strictly reserved from exercising any activity or practice reflecting their own faith or religion at the place of work or within corporate premises.
* **Political Neutrality**: Employees are strictly prohibited from using their professional positions, company resources, or titles to advance their personal political interests or execute discriminatory practices.

## 2. Anti-Bribery and Corrupt Practices
* **Solicitation Prohibited**: Employees shall not solicit or accept any gift, present, financial favor, or hospitality reward having a value in consideration of the technical or professional services they render, or are expected to render, as an employee.
* **Rigorous Penalty**: Any violation of anti-bribery, theft, fraud, misrepresentation, or breach of trust parameters constitutes a major ethical infraction and shall result in immediate rigorous disciplinary investigation and potential dismissal.

## 3. Workplace Misconduct and Harassment
* **Misconduct Definition**: Rigorous disciplinary penalties shall be imposed for severe misconduct, including workplace violence, deliberate procrastination of client cases, reporting to work under the influence of alcohol or drugs, committing immoral acts at the workplace, or abusing corporate power.
* **Sexual Harassment**: The organization operates under a zero-tolerance framework for sexual harassment. Sexual harassment consists of unwelcome sexual advances, requests for favors, or bodily/verbal contact of a sexual nature (e.g., unwelcome kissing, patting, pinching, or path blocking). Engaging in sexual harassment or using a professional position to demand sexual favors as a prerequisite for employment actions (such as promotion, training, or benefits) shall result in immediate termination.
