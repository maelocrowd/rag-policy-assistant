# Travel Policy

## 1. Corporate Travel Scope
This policy governs all local and international travel executed by employees on behalf of NileTech Solutions Ltd. Travel must be explicitly directed by a competent corporate authority to execute official business, client consulting, or institutional representation.

## 2. Travel Accident and Injury Protections
In alignment with the statutory protections of the Federal Civil Servants Proclamation, the company enforces clear safety and health coverage for employees while traveling for work:
* **Commuting and Transport Accident**: Any organic injury or functional disorder sustained by an employee while traveling to or from their place of work—or during official business travel—via a vehicle provided by NileTech Solutions Ltd. or expressly hired by the institution for such purpose, shall be legally classified as an employment injury.
* **Authorized Orders**: Travel injuries sustained outside of regular work hours or regular work locations are fully protected under company medical benefits if the employee was actively carrying out express travel orders given by a competent authority.
* **Exclusions**: Any injury sustained during travel that is caused by the deliberate act of the employee, non-observance of express safety rules, or reporting to travel duties in a state of intoxication caused by drinks or drugs shall be completely excluded from company injury leave and medical benefits.
